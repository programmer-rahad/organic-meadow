ORGANIC GRASS FED MILK

Organic Year Round Grass Fed Milk Is Now Available! Our new organic grass fed milk comes from cows that eat organic grass and dried forages year-round. When cows are fed the diet they evolved to eat, the end result is healthier farms, healthier animals and healthier dairy products.

learn more

ORGANIC MILK

Here is where it all began. Our simple, pure organic milk from cows raised on fresh green pastures using minimal processing. There is a little love in every litre we produce. Pour a tall glass of our cold milk and in a few short seconds you have a great source of protein and vitamin D.

learn more

ORGANIC CREAM

They say cream rises to the top and our cream is way up there. When a product has only one ingredient, organic cream, that one ingredient needs to be creamy and perfect in every way. And our cream over-delivers. So pour some into a steaming cup of coffee or whip it into a delicious dessert and get ready for compliments.

learn more

ORGANIC butter

Our butter begins with pure organic cream and is crafted using traditional time-honored methods. So whatever it ends up be used for - a flaky pie crust, drizzled over popcorn, or spread over a crusty loaf of bread - it often plays a starring role.

learn more

ORGANIC YOGURT

All of our yogurts are made from pure organic milk, with active probiotic cultures for a great start or finish to any day. Whichever type you choose, you are getting a creamy dairy product that is perfect for smoothies, dips and hundreds of other delicious recipe ideas.

learn more

ORGANIC KEFIR

Our organic Kefir is a fermented dairy product that has incredible health benefits. With 2 billion bacterial cultures in one serving, it makes a healthy and beneficial addition to a smoothie or a quick yogurt drink. This wholesome product is available in plain as well as three delicious flavours.

learn more

ORGANIC CREAM CHEESE

Bagels have met their match. Our cream cheese may be a little lighter than most but it is just as incredible and spreadable. So try a cream cheese frosting on carrot cupcakes or a three-layer Mexican dip for a gathering. Our cream cheese is versatile and always a crowd pleaser.

learn more

ORGANIC SOUR CREAM

Our sour cream will be the perfect crown on a steaming hot baked potato. Its creamy texture also adds a cool creamy element to many international dishes. A single dollop is never quite enough.

learn more

ORGANIC COTTAGE CHEESE

Cottage cheese gets its name from the cheese that was made in country homes where milk was easier to get. Today we take our well-loved milk and make a delicious 2% cottage cheese - enjoy it right out of the tub with some fruit or include it in your next lasagna dish.

learn more

ORGANIC CHEESE

Sliced, diced, shredded or melted, cheese connoisseurs will all agree when you craft a cheese using organic milk and nothing artificial, you end up with a delicious addition to many of life’s most pleasurable foods.

learn more

ORGANIC ICE CREAM

Our rich and creamy ice cream is carefully crafted in small batches. Every scoop tells our delicious story again and again - pure simple ingredients that are minimally processed are a real crowd pleaser. Don’t be surprised if they ask for a second bowl. And possibly a third.
