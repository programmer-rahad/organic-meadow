about us

IT ALL STARTS
AT THE FARM

It was over 25 years ago that Organic Meadow pioneered the organic dairy movement in Canada. We brought together like-minded farmers that cared about creating dairy products using nature as their driving force. We continue that same mission today by rolling up our sleeves and crafting wonderful organic dairy products just like we did our first day on the farm.

GROWING AT OUR
OWN PACE

As we patiently evolved our product line, we made sure that our original values were consistent; create exceptional organic dairy products using traditional methods crafted with care. Every new product we bring to our family is made with the same high standards and produced as sustainably as our very first drop of milk.

KEEP IT SIMPLE.
REPEAT.

Today our cows and farmers help us create an entire line of fresh, local dairy products that are minimally processed, from healthy animals that graze on fresh organic pasture. Organic farming may take more work and extra care, but one sip of our fresh milk and you’ll agree, it’s well worth the effort.
