ORGANIC MEADOW PARTNERS WITH GLOBAL
REUSE LEADER, LOOPtm!

As Canada’s first organic dairy, our story is rooted in sustainability. That’s why Organic Meadow is proud to partner with zero-waste leader, Looptm in our continued efforts to reduce waste.

What is Loop tm?

Loop tm is a state-of-the-art reuse shopping platform and home delivery system, designed to eliminate waste by offering consumers their favourite household products in reusable, refillable, zero-waste packaging formats.

Organic Meadow Goes Waste-Free!

As Loop’s first (and only!) dairy beverage partner for their Canadian launch, we are excited to offer our Organic Plain Kefir on the Looptm platform. Proudly packaged in our iconic reusable and refillable zero-waste glass bottle – it’s a sustainable choice you can feel good about.

Reduce, Reuse & Repeat.

A 21st century version of the milkman, the Looptm platform provides a convenient way for consumers to enjoy their favourite products while at the same time eliminating waste.

Get Started!

We believe the future is circular – which is why we are excited to partner with Looptm to reduce waste – one package at a time.

Starting February 2021, Looptm will be available in the Greater Toronto area. We invite you to join us and Go Waste-Free Today!
